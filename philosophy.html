Philosophy

The College of the Immaculate Conception is a Catholic educational institution committed to the integral formation of men and women deeply rooted in faith and Gospel values, and with compassionate awareness of the needs of his fellow men, his society and environment.

Cognizant of the present societal and global challenges, and the diversity in the learners, CIC focuses on the following;

  • Education that centers on the learner
  • Learning that is innovative, stimulating, interesting and fun for the learners
  • Learning that leads to independent life-long yearning for the truth
  • Research programs fostering collaboration and leading to new knowledge/discoveries for the improvement of the quality of life
  • Catholic values that develop keen observance for and foster sensitiveness to one's fellow men
  • Education with a heart that inspires to serve man, nation and nature
